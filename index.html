My Passion

I am Frances,a Nurse specialized in Geriatic care. I am passionate about our older generation and put effort every day to ease the difficulties of the people in my care.

Why Tech?

I developed interest in Tech as a result of the fast evolving Technology all around us,and as path to bring practical idea from the healthcare system and help contribute to the development of applications and softwares that will help ease the workload, hence enabling more time for one-on-one care.

Healthcare challenges today.

Shortage of qualifified workers has increasingly been one of the thoughest challenges faced in healthcare especially in Old Peoples care. Geriatric nursing goes beyond just caring for the aged as proper documentation is highly and essentially necessary. This helps keep track of not just the medical history, but also nutrition and socialization and in turn serves as an emergency document when such arises.
